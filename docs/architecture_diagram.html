Tier 6 — Deployment Strategies

🔵 Blue-Green

Two containers: Blue (stable) + Green (new). Nginx routes 100% → flip instantly. Rollback in <30s.

Zero Downtime

🟡 Canary

Gradual rollout: 5% → 20% → 50% → 100%. Monitor error rate and latency at each stage.

Gradual Rollout

Champion (Production, 100% traffic) vs Challenger (Staging, evaluated). Promote if outperforms.
